About SankarMath - O - Mission

Namaste and welcome to the digital abode of SankarMath - o - Mission, a sacred institution dedicated to the teachings and wisdom of our revered Guru, Sri Srimat Swami Jyotiswarananda Giri Maharaj, affectionately known as Babamani. As a devoted disciple of Babamani, it is an honor to bring you closer to his profound philosophy through this online platform.

Our Mission and Values

Shankar Math and Mission is more than an ashram; it's a spiritual sanctuary, a place of wisdom and enlightenment. Our mission is to propagate the divine essence of AUM and the teachings of the Bhagavad Gita.

We follow the lineage of great spiritual leaders, with Babamani as our guiding light. Our core values revolve around unity, spirituality, and the pursuit of inner peace.

Asrams

Overview:

Welcome to the heart of Shankar Math and Mission, where spirituality finds its home. Our mission is not bound by boundaries, as we stand united in the teachings of Sri Srimat Swami Jyotiswarananda Giri Maharaj, affectionately known as Babamani to his disciples. Babamani's guru, Sri Srimat Swami Swarupananda Giri Maharaj, established this sacred Ashram on Chandranath Pahar, following the divine instructions of his Guru, Sri Srimat Swami Brahmananda Giri Maharaj. This spiritual lineage traces its origins to the revered Swami Satyananda Maharaj, firmly rooted in the Giri community of Adi Shankaracharya.

Our Ashram is a sanctuary of profound spirituality, where devotees come to seek enlightenment, wisdom, and inner peace. We invite you to explore the wisdom of the ages and the timeless teachings of our Gurus.
